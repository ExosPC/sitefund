Our Mission

SiteFund was founded to combat a growing ad-block problem. As more people use ad blockers, this means that website owners bring in less revenue and cannot fund their content. By 2020, $12 billion is projected to be lost to ad blockers.

At SiteFund, we believe that internet users will want to support those website owners given a convenient and inexpensive option. That's why we created an inexpensive subscription service backed by a network of websites promising ad-free content in exchange for the support of our subscribers.

90% of our revenue goes directly to the websites in our network. 5% pays for transaction fees and 5% helps maintain and expand SiteFund.
